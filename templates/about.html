Our History

Born To Lead Mountain Adventure was established by Coach Architect Stephen Muiru as an extension of his pioneering initiative, Born To Lead Academy, founded on July 2, 2016. Driven by a passion for empowering individuals to become their best selves, Coach Stephen created the Academy to offer transformative classes on self-mastery and leadership. However, he envisioned a deeper connection—one that transcended the virtual classroom and fostered both mental and physical wellness. This vision led to the birth of Born To Lead Mountain Adventures, where the wilderness became a classroom, and each hike an opportunity for growth. With over 100 hikes and counting, Coach Stephen continues to inspire and lead others on this extraordinary journey of self-discovery and adventure.

Our Purpose

Born To Lead Mountain Adventure is dedicated to fostering a community where hikers can learn, network, and uplift one another on a journey toward enhanced mental and physical wellness. Through the power of nature, we provide more than just an escape; we offer an opportunity to disconnect from the daily grind and reconnect with oneself and others. Hiking, with its countless benefits—from reducing stress and boosting creativity to building resilience and fostering camaraderie—aligns perfectly with our mission. At Born To Lead Mountain Adventures, we believe that each step taken on the trail is a step toward personal growth, stronger connections, and a healthier, more balanced life..

Meet Our Coach

Stephen Muiru, affectionately known as Coach or Architect, is a multifaceted individual—an accomplished architect by profession and a dedicated life coach by passion. With a deep commitment to helping others realize their full potential, Coach Stephen guides individuals on their journey to achieving their life goals. His wisdom, drawn from a lifelong love of reading, reflects the adage, "A reader is a leader." Coach's philosophy extends beyond mental and professional growth; he champions a healthy, active lifestyle. When he's not leading a hike, you might find him on the tennis court or out for a run, continuously pushing his physical limits. Coach believes that true leadership begins with self-mastery, and through his own example, he inspires others to strive for balance, wellness, and personal excellence in every aspect of life.
